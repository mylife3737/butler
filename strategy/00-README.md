## Glossary

- **HD 75** — Florida House of Representatives District 75 (Charlotte County + southern Sarasota County / Englewood area, post-2022 redistricting).
- **NPA** — No Party Affiliation voter. In FL, NPAs cannot vote in either party's closed primary but can vote in the general. NPAs are the persuasion universe.
- **VBM** — Vote-By-Mail. In FL, VBM requests expire every 2 years (post-SB 90 / 2021). Every door conversation includes a VBM request card.
- **LTE** — Letter to the Editor. Highest-ROI free earned media.
- **VAN / Minivan** — voter contact database & mobile app. Minivan is free to campaigns with VAN access.
- **ActBlue** — the Democratic-aligned online donation processor. Industry standard, no setup fee, ~3.95% processing fee.
- **SOE** — Supervisor of Elections (county-level official who runs the election, maintains the voter file).
- **DS-DE 9** — FL form appointing your campaign treasurer and designating your campaign depository. **Must be filed before you raise or spend any money.** See `11-compliance.md`.
- **Mamdani-style** — shorthand for hyperlocal, authentic, candidate-forward, volunteer-dense, issue-specific grassroots organizing (Zohran Mamdani's 2025 NYC mayoral win being the current reference case).

## Rules of the road

1. **Verify before you ship.** Research files flag ~40% of data points as ⚠️ UNVERIFIED. Close those gaps before any figure appears in a mailer, post, or LTE.
2. **Compliance before anything else.** No raising, no spending, no website launch until `11-compliance.md` checklist is green.
3. **Budget discipline.** Every dollar above $500 needs a written justification tied to a KPI in `09-kpis.md`.
4. **Nothing lives in one head.** Every password, contact, and vendor lives in the shared toolstack described in `12-toolstack.md`.
5. **Candidate time is sacred.** Cynthia's hours per week are in `INTAKE-FORM.md §3.3`. The plan scales to that number; do not overbook her.
6. **Every public attack is sourced.** If we claim Nix voted X, we cite the bill number and vote roll from flsenate.gov / myfloridahouse.gov.
7. **Don't fake authenticity.** Mamdani's model works because it's true. If it isn't Cynthia's voice, it doesn't go out.

## Known realities (don't kid yourselves)

- **District lean:** R+25 to R+30 on present data. A win in 2026 requires either a statewide Democratic wave or extraordinary local collapse of the incumbent. **The default mission is to compete hard, close the margin by 10+ points, build permanent coalition infrastructure, and position for 2028 or for whenever the seat opens.** A win is a stretch goal, not a base case.
- **Budget:** $1,000 total cap. This is a volunteer + earned-media + relational campaign. Paid digital and paid mail are effectively off the table.
- **Team:** minimum viable — Cynthia + 1 volunteer (you) to start. Everything scales from there. Recruitment is step one of every week.
- **Risk of discouragement:** R+28 is hard. Build in monthly wins (see `09-kpis.md`) so the team stays in the fight through November.
