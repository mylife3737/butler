# Week 1 Content Calendar — Public Launch Week

**When this week runs:** Immediately after qualifying (week of ~June 15, 2026), subject to adjustment based on Cynthia's availability (`INTAKE-FORM.md §3.3`).

**Assumption:** Cynthia has completed intake, paperwork is filed, launch event is scheduled for Saturday of this week, and a minimum viable team (Cynthia + manager + comms + field + social) is in place.

**Two parallel paths depending on INTAKE §6.1 answer:**
- **Path V (video-first)** — if Cynthia is comfortable on camera, lead with her voice.
- **Path W (written-first)** — if she's not, lead with written content + photos + third-person narratives, using video sparingly and planned.

Both paths are designed below. **Choose one and execute it consistently** — splitting the difference produces inconsistent content.

## Platform-specific rules

- **Facebook** is the highest-impact platform in HD 75 — older-skewing, engaged, good for events. Post 5x/week, mix formats.
- **Instagram** secondary; cross-post but with platform-appropriate framing (tighter, more visual).
- **TikTok** is optional and entirely dependent on INTAKE §6.1. Do not force it. A bad TikTok hurts more than no TikTok.
- **X/Twitter** is low-leverage for HD 75 but free; auto-cross-post for SEO and press-reporter reach.
- **Threads + Bluesky** optional; auto-cross-post if easy.
- **LinkedIn** — don't bother.
