# Donor Acquisition Plan

**Goal:** build a base of 400 small-dollar donors + 25 mid-dollar ($250+) donors by Nov 3, 2026, giving an average of 2.0+ times each. At an average small-dollar gift of $40, that's ~$35K raised. Combined with mid-dollar, total raise target ~$55K–$75K by election night — enough for yard signs, P2P texting at scale, postage for targeted mail, and a few hundred dollars of paid digital in the final month.

**The $1,000 marketing budget in `08-budget.md` is separate from this fundraising goal.** $1K is the cap on what we spend promoting the campaign; fundraising is what backfills the budget and unlocks incremental tactics.

## Donor universe — segments

### Segment 1 — First 50 (personal network)
- Source: `INTAKE-FORM.md §4.1, §4.2` — Cynthia's top-5-would-show-up list + top-5-would-donate list, expanded to 50 by mining her phone contacts, Facebook friends, church directory, nursing/EMT colleagues.
- Target ask: $50-$250.
- Method: **personal call or text from Cynthia herself.** No email. No form letter.
- Script: see below.
- Close rate target: 50% give, 30% also become volunteers.
- Timing: Sprint 1 (before public launch).

### Segment 2 — Launch-event attendees (70-150 people)
- Source: Saturday launch event RSVPs + walk-ins.
- Target ask: $25-$100.
- Method: ActBlue-powered QR code at the donation table + passing iPads + a paper pledge card.
- Close rate target: 40% of attendees leave with either a gift or a pledge.
- Timing: Launch day (Sprint 2).

### Segment 3 — House-party donors (ongoing, 1/week from Sprint 3)
- Source: hosts invite their personal networks (15-25 per party).
- Target ask: $50-$500.
- Method: host + Cynthia both present; host makes the ask, Cynthia tells her story.
- Close rate target: 60% of attendees donate; average gift $100-$150.
- Cycle goal: 20+ house parties, ~$15K total raised through this channel.
- Playbook: see `06-volunteer-funnel.md` for host-recruitment path.

### Segment 4 — Organic online (email + social)
- Source: email list growth (target 2,500 by Nov) + Facebook followers.
- Target ask: rotating — $15 / $27 / $50 / whatever-you-can.
- Method: end-of-quarter pushes (FEC-style), event-tied pushes (after a debate, after an endorsement, after a news hit).
- Close rate target: 8-12% of email list donates at least once; 40% of those donate more than once.
- Cycle goal: 250-400 online donors.

### Segment 5 — Mid-dollar ($250+) donors
- Source: referrals from house-party hosts, local professionals (lawyers, nurses, retirees with means), union leadership.
- Target ask: $250-$1,000 (FL state-house contribution limit is $3,000 per election — primary and general are separate elections, so $6,000 per cycle theoretically; very few max out). ⚠️ VERIFY 2026 limit with FL DOE handbook.
- Method: coffee meetings (1-on-1 with Cynthia + manager), 30 min each.
- Target: 25 mid-dollar donors by Nov.

### Segment 6 — Endorsement-unlocked donor lists
- Source: Ruth's List Florida, Emerge Florida, and affiliated orgs (see `16-endorsements.md`) maintain donor lists they sometimes fundraise directly for endorsees.
- Method: coordinated email / joint fundraiser after endorsement announced.
- Timing: Sprint 4 onward.

### Segment 7 — National smallest-dollar (longshot)
- Source: when a compelling contrast story travels (e.g., Nix votes for something extraordinarily bad), there's a window to get picked up by national progressive newsletters (Daily Kos "Orange to Blue," DonorsChoose-style newsletter tipsters).
- Do not depend on this. Plan around its absence.

## The first-50 script (Cynthia's personal calls)

**Goal:** raise ~$3,000 and recruit 15 volunteers / hosts before public launch.

```
Hi [Name], it's Cynthia. Got a sec?

[Small talk, if appropriate — this is a real relationship call, not a political call.]

I wanted you to hear this from me before you saw it on Facebook: I'm running for
Florida State House, District 75. Launch is June [date].

[Short version of why — 2 sentences max. Drawn from brand-identity story variant.]

Two reasons I'm calling you specifically:

One, I know you care about [specific thing you know they care about — insurance,
schools, the storms, a neighbor's situation]. That's exactly what this campaign
is about.

Two, I'm not going to win this district without people who actually know me
helping me early. Would you consider donating $100 today so I can get the yard
signs ordered and the website live? Even $25 matters — and if you can't give
right now, would you come to the launch on [date]?

[Listen. Don't over-talk. If they say yes: take the donation NOW via ActBlue link
texted to them while on the phone, OR promise to follow up in 1 hour. If they say
they'll think about it: set a follow-up date.]

One more thing — do you know anyone in [HD 75 city] who should hear about this?
I'm trying to find hosts for living-room events starting in July.
```

**After the call:** log every response in the CRM (Google Sheet for now) with amount pledged, donation date, volunteer offer, house-party interest, and next follow-up date. **No call goes unlogged.**

## ActBlue setup (do this in Sprint 0)

- URL: [actblue.com/entities/add](https://secure.actblue.com/entities/add)
- Required info: campaign legal name, treasurer name + email, campaign depository bank name + routing/account, EIN.
- Fee: 3.95% per donation (withheld automatically).
- Default suggested amounts: $15 / $27 / $50 / $100 / $250. Include a "recurring" option by default — recurring donors are 3x lifetime value of one-time.
- Disclaimer: every fundraising email and every ActBlue form needs the required FL disclaimer (see `11-compliance.md` §Disclaimer Templates).

## End-of-quarter pushes (Q2 June 30, Q3 Sept 30, Q4 after Nov 3)

FEC / FL quarterly reporting deadlines produce natural fundraising moments:

- **Q2 end (June 30):** first real test. Target: $5,000 raised in Q2 total. Message: "Help us report a strong first quarter."
- **Q3 end (Sept 30):** biggest push of the pre-election period. Target $15,000 in Q3. Message: "37 days to go. Close the margin."
- **Election-eve (Nov 2):** final push. Target $5,000 in last week.
- **Thank-you / post-election (Nov 4-15):** win or lose, send thank-you and ask recurring donors to continue at $5/month for list-building beyond the cycle.

## Donor tracking (CRM)

Free tier: a Google Sheet with these columns:
- Name, email, phone, address
- Source (which segment, who referred)
- Gift history: date, amount, channel
- Volunteer flags (doors / phone / data / host / surrogate)
- Notes (what they care about, last interaction)
- Last-contact date
- Next-action date

Upgrade to NationBuilder / NGP VAN / ActionKit only if budget clears $5K — until then, the spreadsheet is enough.

---

## Do not do

- ❌ Purchase email lists (illegal under CAN-SPAM for political, practically useless, reputation risk).
- ❌ Accept corporate contributions (prohibited in FL — F.S. 106.08(1)).
- ❌ Accept anonymous contributions over $50 (prohibited).
- ❌ Accept contributions from out-of-state PACs without vetting them through the treasurer.
- ❌ Spend before you raise. Every check written requires matching funds in the depository.
