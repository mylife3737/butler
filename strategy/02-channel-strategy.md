# Channel Strategy

A marketing matrix for a $0–$1,000 grassroots race. Every channel is ranked by ROI (estimated net persuadable-voter contacts per hour of candidate or volunteer time). Channels with negative ROI in this context are explicitly listed so nobody tries them.

## Explicitly NOT doing on this budget

- ❌ Paid digital ads (Facebook/Google) — below the threshold where $500 buys meaningful reach in an R+28 district. Revisit if budget clears $3,000.
- ❌ Paid direct mail at scale — per-piece cost (~$0.60 with printing and postage) consumes the full budget at a single mailing. Save postage for targeted handwritten.
- ❌ Billboard / transit advertising — cost-prohibitive and low-conversion.
- ❌ Radio or TV ads (paid) — impossibly expensive in the Fort Myers DMA.
- ❌ Robo-dialer voice drops — illegal-adjacent, bad optics, poor conversion.
- ❌ SMS blasting to non-opted-in numbers — TCPA violation risk.

## Audience segmentation

The "persuasion universe" for HD 75 in 2026 is approximately:

- **Base-D turnout** — 15–18K voters likely D at baseline. Don't persuade them; turn them out (VBM chase + GOTV).
- **Persuadable NPA** — 8–12K (⚠️ estimate — refine with book-closing data). The swing. Earned-media + door work here.
- **Soft-R crossover** — 3–6K voters who voted Trump but supported Amendment 4 / Amendment 3 / disliked Wellen Park / had bad insurance experiences. Requires biography + issue-contrast, not partisan appeals.
- **Hard-R base** — do not spend time here. ~25K voters who are voting R no matter what.

Strategy bias: **every channel allocated first to persuasion universe, then to turnout universe, never to hard-R.**

## Weekly cadence (what "shipping" looks like)

- **Monday** — Cynthia records 1 short video clip (selfie or volunteer-shot). Field captain publishes volunteer schedule for the week.
- **Tuesday** — Email sent (supporters + ask).
- **Wednesday** — 1 canvass shift. 1 LTE submitted from a rotating author.
- **Thursday** — Facebook + Instagram posts (event recap or issue explainer). Evening phone bank starting Month 3.
- **Friday** — KPI review (see `09-kpis.md`). Weekend event logistics finalized.
- **Saturday** — Canvass shift. Community-event booth if one is on calendar.
- **Sunday** — Rest day for Cynthia (non-negotiable). Volunteers can still work.

If a week misses 3+ of these, trigger a retro. The campaign dies in the gap between "we planned to post" and "we posted."

---

## Channel ownership

Assign every channel to ONE named person. Channels without owners don't ship. Minimum viable team:

- **Cynthia** — the product; appears in content; owns no operational channels.
- **Campaign manager / Finance chair (can be same person to start)** — treasurer, compliance, budget, donor tracking.
- **Communications lead** — email, LTEs, press pitches, surrogate coordination.
- **Field captain** — doors, volunteers, events, phone banks.
- **Social media volunteer** — Facebook / Instagram / TikTok posting + monitoring.
- **Digital / data lead** — voter file, targeting, CRM, website (can be same as communications to start).

With fewer than 3 people, strategy collapses — see `06-volunteer-funnel.md` for how to recruit to full team by end of Month 1.
