# Endorsements & Coalition Building

Endorsements are force multipliers — every accepted endorsement brings a donor list, a volunteer pipeline, and third-party credibility. In an R+28 district, the candidate CANNOT run without this leverage.

**Target:** 6-10 confirmed endorsements by Nov 3, 2026.

---

## Priority Tier 1 — Apply immediately (Sprint 1, pre-launch)

These organizations run first-time-candidate programs and have firm decision timelines. Missing their spring/summer slate is missing them for the cycle.

### Ruth's List Florida
- **URL:** [ruthslistfl.com](https://ruthslistfl.com/)
- **Focus:** pro-choice Democratic women running for office in Florida at every level.
- **Process:** online application → interview → endorsement decision.
- **Timeline:** decisions typically made in batches through the spring and summer. Apply as soon as DS-DE 9 is filed.
- **Decision-maker:** endorsement committee; staff-led with board sign-off.
- **Unlocks:** endorsed candidates get donor-list access, training, campaign support staff consulting, direct fundraising appeals to the Ruth's List donor base.

### Emerge Florida
- **URL:** [fl.emergeamerica.org](https://fl.emergeamerica.org/)
- **Focus:** recruiting, training, and supporting Democratic women candidates — program is a multi-month training cohort.
- **Process:** application for training program; alumni network then supports each other's campaigns.
- **Timeline:** applications open on rolling cycles. If Cynthia missed the 2025-2026 cohort window, inquire about 2026 mid-cycle support.
- **Unlocks:** training + network + individual donor relationships + staff support.

### Florida Democratic Party (FDP)
- **URL:** [floridadems.org](https://floridadems.org/)
- **Process:** FDP doesn't "endorse" in primaries (typically supports party-nominated candidates), but does support state-house candidates in general elections with voter-file access, coordinated field support, and some financial help.
- **Timeline:** post-qualifying, FDP evaluates which races to invest in. In an R+28 district, don't expect major investment — but VAN access + list-building support is the floor ask.

### Sarasota County Democratic Executive Committee (DEC)
- **Approach:** attend monthly meetings starting April 2026. Request time to introduce yourself. DEC votes endorsement / priority-support status typically after qualifying.
- **Unlocks:** local volunteer base, monthly newsletter distribution, co-sponsored events.
- **⚠️ Find meeting schedule on Sarasota Dems website (URL verify needed).**

### Charlotte County Democratic Executive Committee (DEC)
- **Approach:** same as Sarasota. Charlotte is smaller, more close-knit; personal relationships with chair + vice chair are the key lever.
- **⚠️ Find meeting schedule via charlottedems.com or Facebook.**

### Democratic Women's Club of Sarasota County
### Democratic Women's Club of Charlotte County
- Both chapters run monthly meetings, endorsement interviews, and fundraising events. Local DWC endorsement = immediate donor list + ~20 volunteer signups.

## What to do with an endorsement

Every accepted endorsement triggers a coordinated rollout:

1. **Announce** — press release to Florida Politics + relevant outlets. Cynthia's quote + org's quote.
2. **Social** — social posts across Facebook + Instagram + X + Bluesky the same day.
3. **Email** — blast to full list: "[Org] just endorsed me. Here's what they said."
4. **Fundraising ask** — tied to the endorsement moment.
5. **Volunteer recruitment** — endorsing org's members are the highest-converting new volunteers; ask them specifically.
6. **Joint event** — within 30 days, hold a joint meet-and-greet or canvass kickoff with the endorsing org's members.

## Endorsement questionnaires — preparation

Most questionnaires cover:
- Position statements on key issues (insurance, healthcare, schools, housing, environment, rights).
- Voting record (N/A for Cynthia — first-time).
- Campaign plan (basic).
- Willingness to accept PAC funding (most say yes; Cynthia's call per INTAKE).
- Willingness to support specific bills.

**Strategy:** write Cynthia's position statements ONCE, store in `/Admin/Positions.docx`, paste into each questionnaire. Consistency across questionnaires matters — Florida Phoenix / Herald-Tribune sometimes pull candidate answers side-by-side.

---

## When an endorsement is declined

If Ruth's List or Emerge declines (it happens to strong candidates for calendar / bandwidth reasons):
- Don't make it public.
- Ask for 1-on-1 feedback; often they'll share why.
- Re-apply in Sprint 3 if declined in Sprint 1.
- Continue building the campaign — one declined endorsement doesn't end a candidacy.

---

## What NOT to do

- ❌ Apply to 20 orgs at once. Each needs a tailored application.
- ❌ Accept an endorsement from an org whose other endorsees contradict Cynthia's positions.
- ❌ Announce an endorsement before the org has confirmed the rollout plan with you.
- ❌ Decline a legitimate endorsement for aesthetic reasons.
- ❌ Fail to thank the endorsing org by name every single time you reference them.
